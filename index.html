About Me

Hi! My name is Santai Yang and I'm currently an Applied Physics PhD student at Yale University. I received my Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and Physics with highest honors from University of California, Berkeley in May 2024. My senior honors thesis was completed in Prof. Irfan Siddiqi's Quantum Nanoelectronics Laboratory, where my undergraduate research focused on photon blockade effect in superconducting quantum circuit. My current research focuses on remote entanglement of dual-rail cavity qubits.
